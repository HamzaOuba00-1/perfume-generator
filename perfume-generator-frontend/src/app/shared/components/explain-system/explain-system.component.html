How It Works

A structured and safe approach to essential oil blending

Balanced Notes

Every perfume is built with top, heart, and base notes. This structure ensures a smooth and harmonious evolution over time.

Balanced Composition

Choose at least 3 oils and up to 10 to build a complete formula. Each perfume must include at least one top, one heart, and one base note to ensure balance and structure.

Smart Dosage

Percentages are calculated based on oil intensity. Dominant ingredients are automatically limited.

Reliable Result

The final formula always adds up to 100%. You get a realistic blend ready to be tested.
